Education and working experience

I am looking for a job in the data field, specifically interested in data analysis and engineering.

This page contains several projects that can demonstrate my experience interacting with data, which I have gained over the past 2 years.

My first steps in data analytics began at RANEPA, where I spent 4 years immersed in political economy analysis. After that, I studied thematic courses where I mastered SQL and Python. At the moment, I am getting a master's degree at the University of Messina in Italy, studying AI and deep learning.

I had two years of experience working at VTB Leasing, one of the largest leasing companies in the CIS. My tasks included analyzing the risk indicators of the auto leasing portfolio and developing projects to reduce business risks. In addition, I worked with social networks of one educational project, monitoring the metrics of audience engagement effectiveness.

Projects

Project 1

Having data on the profit from mobile game players, I conducted A/B tests to determine which model of distribution of in-game goods is more profitable. Methods used: Mann–Whitney U test and Bootstrap.

Stack: Pandas, Numpy, Scipy-stats, Seaborn, Matplotlib

Repository in GitHub

Stack: Pandas, Numpy, Scipy-stats, Seaborn, Matplotlib
